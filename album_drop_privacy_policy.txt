Privacy Policy for Album Drop

Last updated: April 2025

---

Overview

Album Drop respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our app.

---

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information. 
Album Drop does not require users to create an account, provide contact details, or submit any personally identifiable data.

---

Tracklist & Artwork

All content you upload — including album artwork or tracklists — is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers. No data is shared, saved, or transmitted externally.

---

Third-Party Services

Album Drop does not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs. The app may include embedded previews of public content (such as album artwork mockups) but does not access streaming accounts or user data.

---

Children’s Privacy

Album Drop is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from them.

---

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
